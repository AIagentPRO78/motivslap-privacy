Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Overview

MotivSlap is a motivational menu bar app for macOS. Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains what data MotivSlap collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data. By downloading, installing, or using MotivSlap, you agree to the terms of this privacy policy.

Data Collection

MotivSlap does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

  • No analytics or tracking. MotivSlap does not include any analytics SDKs, telemetry, or tracking tools.
  • No network requests. MotivSlap does not connect to the internet. It operates entirely offline.
  • No accounts or sign-ins. MotivSlap does not require or support user accounts.
  • No advertising. MotivSlap does not display ads or share data with advertisers.
  • No data sale. We do not sell, rent, lease, or otherwise provide your data to third parties.

Microphone Access

MotivSlap requests access to your Mac's microphone solely to detect physical impacts (slaps or taps) on your MacBook. The microphone audio is:

  • Processed locally and in real-time on your device.
  • Used only to measure sound levels for impact detection.
  • Never recorded, stored, or transmitted. No audio data leaves your device.
  • Never used for voice recognition, speech-to-text, or any form of audio surveillance.

MotivSlap does not capture, log, or retain any audio content. The microphone input is analyzed as numerical sound-level values only, and these values are discarded immediately after processing.

You can revoke microphone access at any time in System Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone. If microphone access is revoked, MotivSlap will continue to function but slap detection will be unavailable.

Local Data Storage

MotivSlap stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your app preferences (sensitivity, cooldown, selected voice, enabled categories).
  • Custom quotes you create.
  • Your motivation count (number of slaps detected).

This data is stored in macOS standard locations (UserDefaults and Application Support) and is never transmitted off your device. This data does not contain any personally identifiable information. Uninstalling the app removes this data.

Data Security

Because MotivSlap does not collect or transmit any data, there is no risk of data breach, unauthorized access, or data loss from our end. All user-generated content (custom quotes and preferences) remains on your local device under the protection of your macOS security settings, including FileVault encryption if enabled.

Third-Party Services

MotivSlap uses no third-party services, SDKs, or frameworks beyond those provided by Apple as part of macOS. We do not integrate with any external APIs, cloud services, or data processors.

Data Retention

MotivSlap retains no data on any server or external system. Local data persists on your device until you uninstall the app or manually delete the data. We have no ability to access, retrieve, or delete your local data remotely.

User Rights

Because MotivSlap does not collect personal data, there is no personal data for us to provide, correct, or delete. However, you retain full control over all locally stored data and may:

  • Delete custom quotes at any time within the app.
  • Reset all preferences by removing the app's data from Application Support.
  • Revoke microphone access at any time via macOS System Settings.
  • Uninstall the app at any time, which removes all associated data.
